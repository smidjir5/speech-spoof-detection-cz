Speech spoof (deepfake) detection for czech language

This is an overview of the progress of diploma thesis aimed at developing a tool for detecting spoofed speech in Czech.

With the advancement of machine learning and artificial intelligence tools, there has also been significant progress in text-to-speech (TTS) and voice conversion (VC) technologies, which can replicate a target voice. These technologies are often exploited—frequently in combination with deepfake videos—for various fraudulent activities targeting businesses, politicians and ordinary individuals.
